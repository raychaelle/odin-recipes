Homemade Noodles

Once you've made these Homemade Noodles, you won't want to go back to store bought. They deffinently add to any dish, but are delicious on their own.

For some reason, making noodles at home is seen as an arduous task. It is seen as a place of mystery for a lot of home cooks. This couldn't be further from the truth, not to mention, kids will love getting their hands in the dough. With only 5 ingredients, noodles are one of the simplest things to make from scratch. If you happen to have a noodle maker on hand it's even easier, but even with a rolling pin and pizza cutter, it's still easy to do.

Indgredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • salt
  • milk

Steps

  1. In a clean bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Whisk the egg in a seperate bowl and add some milk. You can always add more milk later.
  3. Mix the egg and milk into the bowl with the flour mixture. It shouldn't be sticky, but add more milk if it's crumbly.
  4. Once you get the right consistency, sprinkle flour onto a clean surface, and dump the dough into the flour to knead.
  5. Knead dough until smooth.
  6. Seperate into golf ball sized portions.
  7. With a noodle maker, flatten and cut dough to desired size.
  8. In a pan, add broth of your choice and bring to a boil. Then add cut noodles to water and cook for about 5-7 minutes or until firm.

Makes ~8 servings.
