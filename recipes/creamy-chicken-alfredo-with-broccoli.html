Creamy Chicken Alfredo with Broccoli

Creamy Chicken Alfredo with Broccoli is quick and simple and a guaranteed hit. You only need a few ingredients, and less than 30 minutes to put it all together. The juicy chicken mixed with the creamy alfredo sauce makes a heavenly combination. It's a perfect weeknight meal by itself, or can be the main attraction at a dinner party. The chicken and broccoli topped with the savory alfredo sauce will have everyone drooling for more.

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 chicken breasts
  • 1 12 oz. bag of broccoli
  • 1 lb of fettucine noodles (or your choice of noodles)
  • 2 16 oz jars of Ragu original alfredo sauce

Steps:

  1. Fry chicken breasts in pan until no longer pink. Then remove from heat and cut into bite sized pieces.
  2. Return to pan and add both jars of alfredo sauce. Bring to a low boil then reduce down to a simmer.
  3. While chicken is cooking, add fettucine to boiling water and cook as directed on package.
  4. Bring water to a boil in second pan and add broccoli.
  5. Once pasta and broccoli are done, drain both and dump into serving bowl.
  6. Add chicken and alfredo mixture to bowl and combine until noodles and broccoli are evenly coated with sauce.
  7. Eat while hot and put leftovers in refrigerator.

Makes ~12 servings.
